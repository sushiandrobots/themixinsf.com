Presentations

For this event, we’re excited to have the following talks:

  • “The hateful weight” by Henri Helvetica: The web’s growing complexities has made for complex metrics, but the most comprehensible metric remains the page weight. THE HATEFUL WEIGHT looks at image formats and their impact on browsing in a new world.
  • “Design Tokens in Design Systems” by Jina Anne, Lead Designer, Design Systems, Salesforce
  • “Sass community update” by Kaelig, Developer, Design Systems, Salesforce

About Henri Helvetica

Canadian born of Caribbean decent, Henri is a front end dev who has turned his interests to a passionate mix of site performance engineering and pinches of user experience. When not reading the deluge of daily research docs and case studies, programming the Toronto Web Performance Group or DevTO, he’s racking up miles running through the streets of Toronto.
