The Mixin

April 16 2014

Eric Suzanne & Jessica Allen

Come hang out with us for The Mixin, our 4th San Francisco Sass & front-end meet up.

We’ll have a couple talks by 2 rad speakers, plus some food and drinks. Tweeting about this event? Use hashtag: #themixin

Presentations

This time, we’re excited to have the following talks:

  • “DISusy: your layout, our math” by Miriam Suzanne, Artist & experience designer, partner at OddBird, creator of Susy & True, contributor to Compass, and an organizer for the Denver Sass Meet Up
  • “Interactions and animations using Sass with AngularJS” by Jessica Allen, User Experience Designer at Engine Yard

Event Team

  • Jina & Mike — Emcees
  • Salesforce UX — Host & Sponsor
